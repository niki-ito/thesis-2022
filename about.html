「私たちの好き」は中学生向けの、LGBT+アライ(仲間)を育てるスマホ用ブラウザゲームです。LGBT+への正しい知識と理解がまだ少ない中、私たちは日常生活で身近な人を無意識に傷つけていることが多々あるかもしれません。たとえ悪気がなくとも、思い込みによって身勝手な態度を取ると、LGBT+の人にとって苦しい、居心地の悪い環境へとつながりかねません。このゲームは４つのチャプターを４人のLGBT+生徒と進めるノベルゲームです。
プレイヤーのあなたがクラスメイトとなり、会話を通じてLBGT＋生徒が直面する日常生活の問題を学んでいきます。「私たちの好き」は、LGBT+の理解と認識を深めるだけでなく、お互いの「好き」を大切にするためのゲームです。

Our Likes is an immersive story and web-based mobile video game to inspire LGBT+ allies among Japanese middle school students. A survey conducted in 2020 by the Naruto University of Education shows that less than 30% of Japanese middle school students knew the terms “sexual minorities” and “LGBT,” and only 5% knew someone who was a sexual minority. The current lack of LGBT+ awareness among Japanese middle school students perpetuates an unwelcoming environment for queer individuals.
The game takes the player through a series of vignettes with LGBT characters posed as fellow students to simulate conversations that address common issues faced by queer individuals in their environment. Our Likes cultivates the player's LGBT+ awareness while encouraging the essential skill to value the "likes" of their own and others.
